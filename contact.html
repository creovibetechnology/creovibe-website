FAQs

Still Have Questions?

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?
+
We aim to respond to all form submissions within 24 business hours. For the fastest response, we recommend WhatsApp — we typically reply within minutes during business hours (Mon–Sat, 9AM–7PM).
Is the free consultation really free? What does it involve?
+
Yes, completely free — no strings attached. In the 30-minute consultation, we listen to your business goals, analyze your current digital presence, and recommend the best strategy for your specific situation. You leave with actionable insights regardless of whether you choose to work with us.
I'm a school principal. How do I register for the training program?
+
Simply fill out this contact form, select "School Training Program" in the service dropdown, and briefly describe your school and number of students. We'll reach out to schedule a call and plan everything from there. The entire program is free for your school.
Do you work with clients outside your city?
+
Absolutely! Most of our digital services (marketing, web development, AI automation, business consulting) are delivered entirely online. We work with businesses across India. School training programs are currently focused on local schools, but we're expanding — contact us to discuss options.
What information should I prepare before the consultation?
+
It helps to think about: your business goals for the next 6–12 months, your current digital presence (website, social media), your approximate budget range, and any specific challenges you're facing. But don't worry if you don't have all this — we'll guide you through the conversation.
