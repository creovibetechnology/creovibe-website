FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

What makes Creovibe different from other digital agencies?
+
Creovibe is not just a digital marketing or web agency. We are a complete digital growth partner that combines marketing, web development, AI automation, and educational training under one roof. This means you get coordinated, consistent solutions from a single trusted partner instead of managing multiple vendors.
What is the School Training Program and is it really free?
+
Yes! Our 5-Day School Training Program is completely free for partner schools. We visit your school and conduct hands-on digital skills training covering topics like social media, digital safety, AI tools, and career opportunities in the digital world. It's our way of giving back to communities and building a digitally empowered generation.
How much does a website or digital marketing package cost?
+
Our pricing is designed to be affordable and flexible for Indian businesses of all sizes. We offer custom packages based on your specific needs and budget. The best way to get accurate pricing is to book a free consultation where we understand your goals and recommend the most cost-effective solution for you.
How long does it take to build a website?
+
A standard business website typically takes 7–14 working days. More complex projects with e-commerce, custom features, or integrations may take 3–6 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline before starting and keep you updated throughout the process. We don't compromise on quality, but we respect your deadlines.
Do you provide support after the project is delivered?
+
Absolutely. We believe in long-term partnerships. All our projects come with post-delivery support, and we offer ongoing maintenance and growth packages. Our team is available via WhatsApp, email, and phone to address any questions or updates you need.
Can Creovibe help a small business or startup?
+
Yes, and that's actually our specialty! We work with small businesses, startups, local shops, and educational institutes. We understand the challenges of limited budgets and high expectations, so we craft smart, lean solutions that deliver maximum impact without burning through your resources.
