Deep Dive

What Students Will Learn & Experience

📚 Day-by-Day Breakdown

Day 1 — Digital World: How the internet works, websites, apps, and the digital economy. Making students aware of their digital footprint.
Day 2 — Social Media: Using platforms for learning and growth, building a positive online presence, content creation basics.
Day 3 — Cyber Safety: Online safety, recognizing scams, data privacy, responsible digital citizenship.
Day 4 — AI & Tech: What is AI, how to use tools like ChatGPT, the future of jobs, and AI ethics.
Day 5 — Careers: Digital career paths, freelancing, entrepreneurship, how to start building skills today.
